## Project Overview
This project leverages **Tableau** to visualize and analyze sales performance, pricing strategies, and customer distribution across the Arab region (North Africa and the Middle East). The objective is to derive actionable business insights regarding product demand, seasonal fluctuations, and regional market penetration.

## Executive Summary
The analysis focuses on a dataset covering various electronic and educational products. The dashboard highlights key performance indicators (KPIs) to assist stakeholders in decision-making processes regarding inventory management and regional marketing strategies.

### Key Performance Indicators
* **Total Revenue/Price:** 12,337
* **Total Quantity Sold:** 3,335
* **Total Unique Customers:** 310

## Key Insights and Findings

### 1. Product Performance
* **Hardware Dominance:** "Robots" and "Drones" are the leading revenue generators, significantly outperforming software and educational materials.
* **Market Opportunity:** There is a substantial gap between hardware sales and educational kits (Robot Kits, Drone Kits), suggesting a potential for bundling strategies to increase cross-selling.

### 2. Seasonal Trends
* **Q1 Performance:** The data indicates a strong upward trend peaking in March, followed by a significant correction in April.
* **Stability:** The market shows recovery and relative stability moving into May and June, indicating consistent demand post-correction.

### 3. Regional Analysis
* The visualization confirms active market participation in key North African nations (Morocco, Algeria, Egypt) and the Middle East, with varying pricing tiers evident across these borders.
